Fun facts about dogs


1) Similar to humans, dogs can be left- or right-pawed.
2) Dogs can detect human illness, such as cancer by scent.
3) Dogs were the first animal to be domesticated by humans, over 20,000 years ago.
4) Dogs are as intelligent as 2-year-old toddlers and can learn over 100 words and gestures.
5) Dogs see in shades of blue and yellow, not just black and white.
